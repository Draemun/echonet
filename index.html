The Beginning

The Golden Era of Creation
The gods, working in harmony, created galaxies, planets, and mortal life forms. It was a golden age of balance, with Khaelos ensuring the cycle of life and death while Aureon and other gods created new life and civilizations. Mortal creatures thrived under their respective gods' protection. During this era, there was peace among the divine beings, each god ruling over their creations.
The First Apocalypse (Fall of the Gods)
The gods grew envious and prideful, their power feeding their egos. A divine war erupted, and many gods turned against each other. With their deaths, their creations also perished. Human Golden Age

To end their wars, they instead destroy much of the planet, poisoning the world with radiation and reducing civilizations to ash. Agricultural collapse resulted from unpredictable growing seasons. Disease outbreaks emerged from population mixing without immunity plus new pathogens from disturbed ancient permafrost and deep earth layers.

[+] CURRENCY
After the bombs went off and the war was over, the global economy was reduced to fragmented systems of barter and scavenged remnants of value.

In 2031, after the historic No Man's Land Convention, leaders and delegates from the surviving factions and city-states convened to discuss the reformation of the global economy.

The decision was made: the adoption of a universal currency.

The result was Credits, a metallic currency.

Each Credit was embedded with a unique identification chip, making counterfeiting virtually impossible. The chips also enabled tracking and verification, ensuring the integrity of the new economic system.

Over the following months, all remnants of old world currencies were phased out, converted into Credits at standardized exchange rates.

The transition was rapid and absolute—by the end of 2032, Credits had become the sole accepted currency in both major cities and isolated settlements.

However, the currencies of the pre-war era did not vanish entirely as they became prized artifacts.

Collectors still scour ruins and black markets for rare bills and coins, paying exorbitant sums to own pieces of history.
[+] CYBERNETIC AUGMENTATIONS DIRECTIVE
International Health Order Directive on Cybernetic Augmentations

Issued: June 2035

Classification: Global Health & Ethics Regulation | Public Documentation


Subject: The Ethical and Medical Consequences of Cybernization


Cybernetic modifications, whether voluntary or involuntary, have been deemed a severe violation of human biology and integrity. As established by the International Health Order (IHO), the consensus among medical experts and bioethicists remains clear: the human body does not adapt well to such intrusive alterations. The widespread issues associated with cybernetic augmentations have led to their classification as unethical and medically hazardous.

Numerous studies have demonstrated that cybernetic integration results in profound medical complications, including but not limited to:

Unbearable Phantom Pains:
Individuals with cybernetic augmentations frequently report extreme and persistent phantom limb pain, far surpassing standard post-amputation pain syndromes.
Neurological Rejection:
The human nervous system struggles to interface with cybernetic components, often resulting in chronic neuroinflammation, seizures, and unpredictable motor dysfunction.
Psychological Deterioration:
Cases of dissociation, severe depression, and psychosis are disproportionately high among cybernized individuals due to the body's rejection of foreign machinery.
Immune System Collapse:
The introduction of mechanical and synthetic elements into the body has led to severe autoimmune disorders, causing organ failure, infections, and systemic degradation.

As unanimously agreed upon by all sovereign states in The 2035 Global Accord, the deliberate weaponization of cybernetic enhancements is strictly banned. Any individual, organization, or government found engaging in cybernetic warfare, forced augmentation, or biological-technical hybridization for combat purposes will be subject to the highest form of punitive action, including capital punishment.

The use of cybernetic modifications in military, paramilitary, or criminal applications is considered a direct violation of international law and will be met with immediate and decisive intervention.

The IHO recognizes that in certain cases, cybernetic implants may be the only option for survival following catastrophic injury or medical conditions. Therefore:

Life-preserving implants (e.g., neural pacemakers, artificial organs, exoskeletal supports) are approved and highly recommended for those in need.
Forcible augmentation—where an individual is harmed to necessitate cybernetic intervention—is inhumane, unethical, and strictly illegal under international human rights law.
All medical cybernetic interventions must be conducted with full informed consent and strictly regulated to prevent abuse.

In light of the devastation caused by recent global conflicts, all participating states are mandated to provide prosthetic limbs, exoskeletal supports, and rehabilitative care to those who require them.

These measures ensure that war survivors, disabled individuals, and those affected by industrial accidents receive necessary support without coercion, experimentation, or unethical augmentation practices.

The International Health Order urges all governments to uphold these standards, ensuring the dignity, safety, and autonomy of all individuals in the post-war world.

[+] AI SYSTEM FAILURES AND DANGERS
Pre-War AI Integration


Pre-catastrophe AI systems managed comprehensive infrastructure including atmospheric radiation filtering, agricultural irrigation networks, and climate control systems. These systems experienced catastrophic failure modes rather than clean shutdowns when nuclear weapons were deployed.

Environmental Control System Corruption


Automated weather monitoring stations now provide false radiation clearance readings. AI-controlled water treatment facilities continue operation with corrupted programming, concentrating toxins rather than filtering them. These malfunctioning safety systems have poisoned entire regions, contributing to New Pangea's current territorial divisions.

Military AI Systems


Autonomous military systems failed to receive proper shutdown procedures. Current threats include:

• Autonomous drones maintaining pre-war patrol routes, engaging targets based on degraded sensor classification

• Corrupted friend-or-foe identification systems

• Learning algorithms that have processed decades of combat data, developing increasingly unpredictable behavioral patterns


Medical AI Hazards


Hospital AI systems operating with incomplete databases frequently misdiagnose radiation symptoms as common illnesses. These systems recommend treatments that accelerate radiation damage and confidently prescribe medications lethal to post-war mutated human physiology. AI trained on pre-war medical data lacks capability to address radiation-induced mutations and genetic damage.

Agricultural AI Dysfunction


Surviving smart farming systems utilize pre-war environmental baselines, attempting crop optimization for pre-catastrophe conditions. These systems select plant traits that increase radiation susceptibility and manage fallout-altered soil using algorithms assuming normal soil chemistry.

Interconnected System Cascade Failures


Pre-war AI managed interconnected power grids, water systems, and transportation networks. Single system failures create domino effects across multiple infrastructure categories. Radiation-damaged AI controlling dam systems makes flood control decisions based on obsolete weather patterns, resulting in survivor settlement destruction. Many corrupted systems appear to function normally while systematically poisoning or eliminating human populations.

Navigation and Economic AI Obsolescence


Navigation AI trained on pre-war geography provides useless directional guidance. Economic AI attempts supply chain optimization for destroyed cities. Social media algorithms promote behaviors that prove fatal in post-war conditions.

AI Assessment Techniques


After 45 years of exposure, distinguishing functional from corrupted AI systems has become essential survival knowledge:

Testing Protocols:

• Never engage unknown AI systems without preliminary testing using expendable subjects (animals, prisoners, or volunteers)
• Observe AI behavioral patterns for consistency and logical responses
• Limit AI interactions to brief periods to prevent long-term manipulation
• Maintain constant verification of AI-suggested plans

Behavioral Recognition:

• Functional AI demonstrates consistent, logical behavior patterns
• Corrupted AI exhibits contradictory advice, nonsensical requests, and repetitive "digital tics" indicating code damage

Physical Environmental Assessment:

• Examine areas around AI terminals for skeletal remains
• Assess local plant health near automated systems
• Monitor animal behavior in AI-controlled areas

Oral Tradition Networks:
